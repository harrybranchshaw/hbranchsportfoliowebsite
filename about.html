Student-Athlete, Outdoorsman, Designer. Carnegie Mellon University School of Architecture.

Born in 1998, I grew up in the East Village of NYC. My parents are very art oriented, and I was born into a creative family. Began making art at a young age, but soon became captivated by architecture and furniture. I find inspiration in all of my outdoor pursuits. Much of what I design is inspired by my passion for nature and the outdoors, particularly surfing, running, and skating.
